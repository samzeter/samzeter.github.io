General pedal building notes

Drilling

Think about battery placement Don’t squish pots and LED 125b Are too small for starter builds (bigger the better)

General Don’t bother with battery snaps or bezels Buy all components first Add diode protection to circuits? Add Rb protection to circuits? Put dc jacks at top no the side Use a throw switch on a box (instead of stomp) Use screwable LEDs if possible and if using superglue, only a dab — absolute pain if the LED needs replacing Label what pots are for what (Volume etc) on the PCB to avoid having to look back at designs.
